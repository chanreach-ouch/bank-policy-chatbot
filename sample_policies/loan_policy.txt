Loan Policy Summary (Version 3.0)

1. Personal loan applicants must be at least 21 years old.
2. Minimum monthly income threshold:
   - Salaried: USD 400
   - Self-employed: USD 600 with verifiable records
3. Mandatory documents:
   - National ID or Passport
   - Income evidence (salary slips or tax filings)
   - Recent bank statement (last 3 months)
4. Standard loan processing time is 3 to 5 business days after complete document submission.
5. Early settlement fee may apply depending on product terms.
6. Final approval remains subject to internal risk assessment and compliance checks.
